IVE Live @ Indoor Stadium

Live Nation Singapore is thrilled to announce that one of the fastest-growing K-Pop groups, IVE, have announced their first global outing – 1ST WORLD TOUR ‘SHOW WHAT I HAVE’. The 24-date world tour will hit various cities across Southeast Asia, North America, Europe, the U.K., South America, Eastern Asia and Australia next year. IVE THE 1ST WORLD TOUR IN SINGAPORE will take place at Singapore Indoor Stadium on 24 February 2024.
